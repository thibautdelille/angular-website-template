Pokemon Black and White 2

View the site

After multiple successfull small projects for Pokemon, I got the chance to work on the Pokemon Black and White 2 Website back in 2012. Pokemon came to us and let us free range of creativity for the project. Creative and Developers came up with a double parallax site that will confront the Black side to the White side.

For this site I developed a custom Javascript frameworks based on JQuery. Here is a list of the different featured of the site:

  • XML Generated Site
  • EFIGS Languages authored with XML
  • Image Preloading
  • Multi Directional Parallax System
  • Deep Linking
  • Agegate
  • Page Transitions

Pokedex 3d Pro

View the site

Our friends at Pokemon needed our help to create a website for the last version of the Pokedex 3d Pro game. The objective of this site was to create a semi 3d environment on the site. Not an Easy task when you have to support ie7. I developed a custom frontend frameworks based on JQuery that use a parallax systems to do the trick

Like all the Pokemon site this site support 12 differents Languages, witch doesn't make the task easier ;)
